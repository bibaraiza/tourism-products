What are Tourism Products?

Tourism products are services and experiences offered to tourists. Below are simple examples of tourism products that can be offered in the Philippines.

Examples of Tourism Products

Boracay Beach Tour

A beach tour experience featuring white sand beaches and island hopping.

Sample Price: ₱5,000

Palawan Island Tour

An island tour showcasing lagoons, clear waters, and natural scenery.

Sample Price: ₱8,000

Intramuros Cultural Tour

A cultural tour that introduces Philippine history and heritage.

Sample Price: ₱1,200

Bohol Countryside Tour

A nature tour featuring the Chocolate Hills and river cruise.

Sample Price: ₱3,000

Importance of Tourism Products

Tourism products help promote destinations, support local businesses, and contribute to the Philippine economy.
